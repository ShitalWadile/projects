Here are some frequently asked questions !

1. How do you calculate my home loan eligibility?

Mostly all the banks offer a home loan amount that is up to 60 times your existing net income per month. Also, banks do not take into consideration leave or medical allowance while making the calculation. Banks also take into consideration your CIBIL (credit) score and also your commitments to other EMIs (if any). Therefore, you need to understand what exactly your lender is looking for while calculating your home loan eligibility.

2. What would be an ideal home loan tenure ?

Generally, most home loan lenders provide a tenure that varies from 1 to 30 years for loan repayment. However, the flexible home loan tenure can be tweaked as per your income, age and financial situation. Therefore, it is important to askyour lender about the tenure range available, as your home loan EMIs will completely depend on the tenure you choose.

3. What is the home loan processing fee?

We don't charge any loan processing fee like banks do. You can complete the whole home loan process with free of cost.

4. What is the interest rate offered on home loan?

Even a minor difference in interest rate can have a huge impact on the overall loan amount and EMIs. So that's why we offer 8.5% of rate on the whole loan amount.

5. What are the documents required to apply for a home loan?

There are certain generic documents like Sale Deed, Possession certification, NOC, etc, and KYC documents such as Aadhar Card, PAN Card Voter ID Card, etc. that anyone applying for a home loan must provide. Besides these,salaried professionals and self-employed individuals need to share some specific documents depending upon their nature of work.
